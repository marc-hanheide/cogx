\page main_recogniser Recogniser Component

\section desc_recogniser Description

The \p Recogniser component is a managed (goal driven) component that determines the object's visual attributes from the its visual features. The component maintains visual knowledge in the form of concepts that map features to attributes. The concepts are stored in the form of Kernel Density Estimations and are continuously updated. See \subpage main_featureLearning.


\section conf_recogniser Configuration

\n

Options: \n
