Utterance planner
input-->output LF

Examples:


Adding or changing nominal sort

IN1: @b1(ball)
In2: @b1:entity(ball)
OUT: @b1:physical(ball)

Adding propositions: like before
Adding feature/value pairs: like before
Adding/adjoining LF: like before

Creating a new discourse referent
IN:  @b1(ball)
Out: @s1:perception(see ^ 
                 <Actor>(i1:person ^ I ^ 
                         <Num>sg) ^ 
                 <Patient>(b1(ball)) )
or
IN:  @b1(ball)
OUT:  @b1:entity(ball ^ <Modifier>(p1:q-color ^ red))

Replacing feature/value
IN: @b1(ball ^ <Quantity>1)
OUT: @b1(ball ^ <Num>sg ^ <Quantification>specific)
[Note: Input feature, e.g., <Quantity>, may be needed in several systems]

Changing relation

IN: @b1:entity(ball ^ <Color>(p1 ^ red))
OUT: @b1:entity(ball ^ <Modifier>(p1:q-color ^ red))

In:  @b1:entity(ball ^ <Left-of>(n1 ^ box...) )
OUT: @b1:entity(ball ^ <Modifier>(n1:m-location ^ left/right/near ^ 
              <Anchor>(b2:thing ^ box ...)))
